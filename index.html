About Street Smarters

Our Mission

To rescue, shelter, educate, and nurture vulnerable children in Busia and Western Kenya — providing a safe, loving, faith-filled environment where every child can heal, grow, and discover their God-given potential.

Our Vision

A Kenya where no child sleeps hungry, goes without schooling, or grows up without love. We envision a generation of confident, educated, spiritually grounded young people who lift up their families and communities.

Our Story

Street Smarters Rescue Centre was founded six years ago by Doris Wako, a woman who saw children living on the streets of Busia and refused to walk past them. Starting with just a few children, a rented room, and a heart full of faith, Doris built what is now a thriving home for over 30 children. Today, every child at Street Smarters attends school, eats three meals a day, and goes to sleep in a safe bed — because of the tireless work of our small team and the generosity of supporters near and far.

What We Do

Our Programmes

Six years of care built on four pillars — every programme designed around the whole child.

🏠

Safe Housing

A clean, safe, and loving home environment for children removed from dangerous situations. Every child has a bed, clean clothes, and a space they can call their own.

📚

Education

We pay school fees and provide uniforms, books, and supplies. Older children support younger ones with homework — building both skills and bonds.

🍽️

Nutrition

Three nutritious meals every day. A full stomach is the foundation of everything else — focus, energy, healing, and joy.

🏥

Healthcare

Regular medical check-ups, access to medicine, and mental health support for children who have experienced trauma.

✝️

Faith & Character

Weekly devotions, prayer, and values-based mentoring. We raise children who know they are loved by God and equipped to love others.

🎨

Arts & Play

Sports, music, dance, art, and outings. Children need joy — and at Street Smarters, joy is never in short supply.

✝️

Rooted in Faith, Driven by Love

Street Smarters Rescue Centre is a Christian organisation operating under Hope In Christ Africa. Our work is guided by the belief that every child is made in the image of God and deserves to be loved, protected, and given the chance to flourish.
